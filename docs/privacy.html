Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Finance Hub ("we", "our", "the app") is a desktop application for Windows that provides real-time market data visualization. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Finance Hub does not:

  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Track user behavior or usage analytics
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Store any data on external servers

3. Local Storage

The app stores user preferences (watchlist, alerts, settings) locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your computer.

4. Third-Party APIs

Finance Hub fetches market data from public financial APIs (Yahoo Finance, Financial Modeling Prep, Finnhub, FRED). These requests contain only the financial instrument symbols being queried — no personal information is transmitted.

5. No Advertisements

Finance Hub does not display advertisements and does not share data with advertising networks.

6. Children's Privacy

Finance Hub is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
