## Description (for Product Hunt page)

Finance Hub is a free, open-source desktop app for Windows that gives you a Bloomberg Terminal-like experience — without the $20,000/year price tag.

**What it does:**
- Tracks stocks, crypto, forex, and commodities with real-time updates
- 30+ professional technical indicators (Volume Profile, Ichimoku, VWAP, Fibonacci — features that cost $15-60/month on TradingView)
- AI-powered Market DNA: regime detection, conviction scoring, and automated market narratives
- Multi-source news aggregation with sentiment scoring
- Smart price alerts with conditional logic (e.g., "Alert me when BTC > $70K AND VIX > 20")
- Beautiful dark UI with glassmorphism, spring animations, and aurora backgrounds

**Why it exists:**
We believe market intelligence shouldn't be locked behind expensive subscriptions. Finance Hub puts Wall Street-grade tools in the hands of everyday investors — for free.

**Tech stack:**
Electron 28 + vanilla JavaScript. No React, no heavy frameworks. Just fast, responsive market data visualization.

**No account required. No ads. No tracking. Just open it and see the markets.**
