Finance Hub

Privacy Policy — Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Finance Hub is a desktop application for Windows that displays real-time stock market data and financial information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the application uses and how it is handled.

Data We Do Not Collect

Finance Hub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are sent to our servers
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties

Market Data

Finance Hub retrieves publicly available financial market data from third-party providers including Yahoo Finance. This data retrieval is read-only and does not involve transmitting any personal information.

Local Storage

Any user preferences or settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The application connects to the following third-party services to retrieve market data:

  • Yahoo Finance — stock prices and financial data

Contact

If you have any questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
